About DEI 2024

Following the success of the 2019 International Conference at Said Business School, University of Oxford, we proudly present the International Conference on "Diversity, Equity and Inclusion: Creating Value-Based Sustainable Organizations."

The conference is a crucial forum dedicated to discussing high integrity and high-responsibility organizations, emphasizing fair practices, responsible decision-making, and enlightened corporate citizenship. It highlights the creative role of spirituality in economic decisions and the necessity of non-materialistic value orientation for real material wellbeing.

**Focus:** The three-day event will provide a platform to discuss strategies for implementing diversity, equity, and inclusion (DEI) initiatives to create happy, harmonious, and sustainable workplaces that utilize the full potential of employees.

Call for Papers

We invite academicians, research scholars, entrepreneurs, and business leaders to submit abstracts and full papers on the following thematic areas.

1. Human Values and Ethics in Organizations

Focus on values, culture, and organizational behavior.

  • Role of human values in creating happy organizations
  • Indian Ethos in Management
  • Managerial effectiveness and quality of work life
  • The ethics of decision making

2. DEI: A Human Consciousness Approach

Insights from scriptures and corporate experiences.

  • Integrating Spirituality and Organisational Leadership
  • Happiness, Virtues, Well-being and Flourishing Organizations
  • Love and Truth: New Wave in Organizational Development
  • Collective Pride and Harmony in the Workplace

3. DEI: Sustainable Business Practices

Sharing and discussing practices to promote sustainability.

  • Sustainability - Leadership
  • Innovative Business Models and SDGs
  • Sustainable Reporting & Responsible Business Conduct
  • Consciousness, Sustainability, and Natural Law

4. DEI for Corporate Social Justice

Cases and reflections on social justice at the workplace.

  • Panel Discussion on Corporate Social Justice
  • Corporate Case Presentations on Sustainable Business Practices
  • Doctoral Student Presentations

5. Promoting Care Democracy in Workplaces

Addressing challenges in post-Covid times.

  • Workplace Democracy vs Care Democracy
  • Humanity Deficit at Workplace: The Unheard Voice
  • Gender, Care and Responsibility
  • Role of Employer/Leader in promoting Care Democracy

6. Indigenous Management Models

Models for creating sustainable and inclusive organizations.

  • Experience Sharing
  • Case Study Presentation
